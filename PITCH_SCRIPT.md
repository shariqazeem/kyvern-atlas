## Reading discipline

- Eye contact with the lens, not the screen.
- ~135 wpm — slightly slower than the demo. Pauses earn weight.
- Don't end any line with rising intonation. Flat or descending.
- The three pauses inside Beat 3 ("Not a server. Not a heuristic. Consensus.") are the most important beats in the whole pitch. Trust them. Don't rush.
- If you lose your line mid-take, breathe, restart the beat from the top, keep rolling. Editor can splice.
